Students must create a website from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JS that delivers information important for prospective members. This website must be visually appealing and have a home page, about us page, and info page. The about us page contains member names and pictures, information about the program, and teacher and school recognition. In addition, the info page contains instructions on how to prepare for and join the club.
