Our Mission

TEAMNAME, the official Robotics Team of ACCEL Middle College, is a student-led organization that enables students to explore STEM pathways through hands-on mechanical and software design and development. We learn and practice engineering principles and techniques over the course of designing, building, and programming our competition-ready robot. Founded on principles of dedication, innovation, and collaboration, our team is open to all students of ACCEL Middle College (and some East Side Union High School District students), regardless of prior experience.

AMC Robotics

ACCEL Middle College Robotics is a VEX Robotics Team founded in 2020. TEAMNAME, also known as VEX Team 47731M, are a dedicated group of students passionate about technology and design. We are supported by a community of students committed to the success of our fledgling team as we begin our VEX journey.

ACCEL Middle College

Accel Middle College Program is a cooperative program between East Side Union High School District and Evergreen Valley Community College, and the home of the TEAMNAME. As a student-run club, our team embraces ACCEL Middle College’s mission to provide students who are capable of challenging themselves in a college environment with equal access to quality education and opportunity in a supportive, encouraging environment. To learn more about ACCEL Middle College, check out our Information page!

Challenges

We are embarking our VEX journey by participating in two online competitions – Dell Website Challenge and Texas Instruments Electronics Challenge. Given the current situation with the COVID-19 Pandemic, we are unable to meet and build a robot this season. These online challenges have given us an opportunity to still collaborate as a team with different challenges suited to be done online. To learn more about how we work online and see some of our work, please check out our Information page!
